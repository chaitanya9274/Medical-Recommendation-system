In the age of technology and information, access to accurate and timely healthcare is more critical than ever. With the growing importance of remote healthcare solutions, we built a symptom-based disease diagnosis web app. Powered by Flask and a Decision Tree Classifier model, this platform helps users identify potential illnesses based on their reported symptoms.

The Problem

Many people experience symptoms but struggle to identify the underlying condition. With overlapping symptoms across diseases, quick, accessible, and reliable health insights are needed.

The Solution

The web app guides users to input their symptoms and predicts possible diseases using machine learning. Here‚Äôs the process:

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter symptoms through a clean and interactive interface.
  2. Machine Learning Model: A Decision Tree Classifier, trained on a dataset of symptoms and diseases, makes predictions.
  3. Prediction: The app provides a probable disease instantly.
  4. Additional Information: It also shows disease descriptions, precautions, medications, diet tips, and workouts.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Smooth navigation and intuitive design for quick input.
  • Accurate Predictions: Reliable results from the trained ML model.
  • Comprehensive Guidance: Beyond prediction, it offers lifestyle and health suggestions.
  • Educational Content: Blog posts help raise awareness about health and wellness.

Conclusion

The Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis App combines technology and healthcare to give users quick, reliable, and informative health insights. This project represents a step forward in making healthcare accessible, personalized, and user-centric.
